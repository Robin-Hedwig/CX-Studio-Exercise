Mid and Senior AWS Developer

  • Strong knowledge of AWS services and cloud architecture.
  • A strong understanding of the way the web works.
  • Functional understanding of agile methodologies such as Scrum.
  • Able to provide and accept constructive feedback.
  • Ability to collaborate with and support other team members.
  • A mindset that seeks to understand before prescribing a solution.

  • Experience with integrating systems with Generative AI
Role in the organisation
  • Mid Engineers are pivotal members of our delivery teams, bridging the gap between junior talent and senior expertise. You will collaborate closely with our solution architects and senior engineers to execute designs and enhance our overall product. With a continuous learning mindset, you will actively contribute to refining our delivery processes, embrace challenges that come with system development, and seek mentorship to grow professionally. Your feedback is invaluable, and you'll have opportunities to voice your experiences and provide input to improve our collaborative environment.
  • Senior Engineers are considered the backbone of our delivery teams. You would be the main builder of the solutions designed by our solution architects and the greater team. You will work to refine the process of delivering projects by building internal tools, suggesting new methods of designing and building a system and providing feedback to the team leads on issues they are experiencing.
Day to Day
  • We know that developing software takes concentration and that meetings are frustrating to developers who need to deliver a system. So, we encourage our PMs to have group meetings in the mornings to allow for open afternoons for continuous development work.
  • Morning meetings with the internal team and clients.
  • Requirements analysis and design conversations when you have a new project starting.
  • Working on issues in Jira with your team - building systems, creating CloudFormation/CDK/Terraform templates to deploy resources, etc.
  • Learning best practices used in the NodeJS, JavaScript/TypeScript, and Python communities.
  • Working with your mentor/team leads to further knowledge of AWS Services, tools, or even new languages to address project needs.
  • You'll need to be eager to learn and develop your skills to support the team and customers.
